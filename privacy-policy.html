Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Green Landscaping ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses Google reCAPTCHA to protect our contact form from spam and abuse. reCAPTCHA sets cookies on your device to verify that you are human and to prevent fraudulent submissions. By using our website, you consent to the use of these cookies.

We do not use cookies for any other purpose at this time. No analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies are set on this website.

Your Information

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • First and Last Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number (optional)
  • Service Required
  • Message and any uploaded photos

This information is sent to our email inbox via Web3Forms and is used only to respond to your inquiry and provide landscaping services. We do not share your information with third parties.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us via our website.
